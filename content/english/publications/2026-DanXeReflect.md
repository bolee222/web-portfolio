DanXeReflect is an XR system that transforms rehearsal video into an interactive 3D studio where past movements appear as avatars for collaborative reflection. Dancers can re-enact poses to search sequences, demonstrate alternatives for embodied revision, and attach body-anchored annotations via drawing or voice. The system supports asynchronous reflective practice by reconnecting annotation and feedback to spatial-temporal movement context.
