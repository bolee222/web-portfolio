**DanXeReflect: Interacting with the Spatio-Temporal Past Movements for Embodied, Reflective Choreographic Collaboration** received an **Honorable Mention Award (Top 5%)** at the ACM CHI Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems 2026.

Developed by **Hyunju Kim, François Guimbretière, and Bokyung Lee**, DanXeReflect transforms rehearsal videos into an interactive XR studio. Dancers and choreographers can revisit past movements as 3D avatars, search sequences through embodied pose reenactment, demonstrate alternative movements, and leave body-anchored annotations for reflective collaboration.

The work was conducted in collaboration with Cornell University and a dance studio in New York, grounding the system in real-world choreographic practices.
