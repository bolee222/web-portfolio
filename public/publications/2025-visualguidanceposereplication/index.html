A Preliminary Study on Visual Guidance in Pose Replication

  • read

This preliminary study explores how different visual guidance designs affect users’ ability to replicate poses in photo-taking scenarios. It discusses implications for improving accuracy, usability, and satisfaction in pose-based interaction systems.
