Me.

Hi. I am an Interaction Design / HCI researcher. I hold a Ph.D. in Industrial Design at KAIST (Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology). I am currently working as a Post-Doctoral Fellow at Autodesk Research, Toronto.

My research lies at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction and Interactive System Designs. I envision the potential interactions for future technologies (VR/AR/AI) with a goal of enhancing our experiences. During my Ph.D., I explored embodied interactions for VR/AR digital design tools. I enjoy creating and applying new user-centered design methods, as well as prototyping interactive systems. To read my entire research vision, please visit here.
